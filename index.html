Chemical Engineer

I graduated from chemical engineering and worked in the field for a while before pursuing web development. Working as an engineer let me develop skills that I use every day during coding:

As part of my chemical engineering degree, I had the opportunity to work a few co-op terms. During my terms, I worked at Hatch Ltd in risk management and site deployment. I had the privilege of participating in numerous contingency analyses for project costs and scheduling, often contributing to the discussion with practical analysis. This skill lets me breakdown complex problems into smaller problems that I can tackle one a time - extremely useful for daunting tasks like building a website.

Additionally, after graduating I began working at a gas sensor manufacturing company as a quality assurance and calibration engineer. After the gas sensor units were built, I was responsible for calibrating each sensor to accurately measure gas concentrations as well as ensuring that there were no other quality issues with the product. I took the initiative and developed an excel tool to automate much of the process to significantly reduce the time it took to calibrate each unit so that the products could more reliably meet shipping deadlines. I also acquired valuable hands on experience with problem solving, test procedure development and troubleshooting as I evaluated the range of products at the company.

This website was built with the help of Grayscale. Grayscale is a beautiful Bootstrap theme created by Start Bootstrap.
